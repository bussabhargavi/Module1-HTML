Exercise 8: Help Page

Welcome to the help page of the Community Event Portal. Here you can find answers to common questions and tips on using the site.

How to Register for Events

  • Fill out the registration form on the Home page.
  • Make sure to provide a valid email address.
  • Choose your preferred event type from the dropdown.
  • You can optionally enter a phone number.
  • Click Submit to register.

Using the Event Gallery

Double-click any event image to enlarge it for better viewing.

Feedback and Contact

Use the feedback section to send us your comments or questions. The character count below the textarea helps you track your message length.

Video and Geolocation

  • Watch the promo video on the home page for event highlights.
  • Click "Find Nearby Events" to allow the website to detect your location and show events near you.

Clear Preferences

You can clear your saved event preferences by clicking the "Clear Preferences" button on the registration form.
